About the Game

Jack Toresal is a street orphan living by his wits in the medieval town of Toresal. When a dying stranger entrusts him with a mysterious letter, Jack is drawn into a web of intrigue involving nobles, spies, and a secret that could change the kingdom forever.

Originally released in 2009 by Textfyre, this richly illustrated interactive fiction game has been lovingly restored for modern browsers and desktop platforms using OpenSilver and Tauri.

Story by David Cornelson. Written by Michael Gentry. Sketches by Erika Swanson.
